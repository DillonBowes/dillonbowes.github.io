How Wars Affect the Average Investor

Markets during war are wild. Some companies tank, some boom, and the whole economy can feel unpredictable. But there are patterns: defense contractors, energy companies, food suppliers, and cybersecurity firms often outperform.

I looked at historical examples from WWII to today and found that if you understand which sectors get government spending or see spikes in demand, you can make smarter moves even as a regular investor.
